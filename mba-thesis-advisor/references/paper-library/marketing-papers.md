### 理论：品牌资产模型 (Brand Equity Model)

- id: "aaker-1991-book"
  role: "origin"
  title_en: "Managing Brand Equity: Capitalizing on the Value of a Brand Name"
  authors: ["David A. Aaker"]
  year: 1991
  source: "The Free Press"
  contribution: "Pioneered the concept of brand equity, defining it as a set of brand assets and liabilities linked to a brand, its name and symbol, that add to or subtract from the value provided by a product or service."
  cite_gbt7714: "[1] Aaker, D. A. Managing Brand Equity: Capitalizing on the Value of a Brand Name[M]. New York: The Free Press, 1991."
  cite_apa: "Aaker, D. A. (1991). Managing brand equity: Capitalizing on the value of a brand name. The Free Press."

  authors: ["Kevin Lane Keller"]
  year: 1993
  source: "Journal of Marketing"
  contribution: "Introduced the Customer-Based Brand Equity (CBBE) model, defining brand equity as the differential effect of brand knowledge on consumer response to the marketing of the brand. This model provides a framework for building and managing brand equity from the consumer's perspective."
