- id: "modigliani-miller-1958"
  role: "origin"
  title_en: "The Cost of Capital, Corporation Finance and the Theory of Investment"
  authors: ["Franco Modigliani", "Merton H. Miller"]
  year: 1958
  source: "The American Economic Review"

  authors: ["Michael C. Jensen", "William H. Meckling"]
  year: 1976
  source: "Journal of Financial Economics"
  contribution: "This paper develops a theory of the ownership structure of the firm based on agency costs. It explains how a firm's financing choices can be affected by conflicts of interest between managers, shareholders, and debtholders."
  cite_gbt7714: "[1]JENSEN M C, MECKLING W H. Theory of the firm: Managerial behavior, agency costs and ownership structure[J]. Journal of Financial Economics, 1976, 3(4): 305-360."
